How to be Awesome

Because you know you've always wanted to be...

Here are a few things you can do right away to be awesome:

  1. Upon meeting people, let them know you are awesome
  2. Afterwards, do not let people forget that you are awesome
  3. Being right is awesome, know that you are right
  4. If you're not right, you're not listening to point 3
  5. Occasionally, some people will doubt you, just ignore them, they're wrong
  6. Make awesome lists about how to be awesome

Once you've gotten that down, you may be ready for advanced techniques:

  • Associate with someone who is awesome (if I'm not around find someone kind of awesome)
  • Convince people your whip is awesome (even if it's just a huffy)
  • Act like me (I'm pretty much the epitome of awesomeness)
